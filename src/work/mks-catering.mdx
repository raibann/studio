## The Challenge

MKS Catering needed a complete digital transformation. Their manual ordering process was causing delays, miscommunications, and frustrated customers. They needed a platform that could handle everything from menu browsing to order tracking.

## Our Solution

We built a comprehensive catering management platform that includes:

- **Online Ordering System** — Customers can browse menus, customize orders, and place them directly through the website
- **Admin Dashboard** — Real-time order management, inventory tracking, and delivery scheduling
- **Customer Portal** — Order history, favorites, and automated notifications

## The Result

The new system reduced order processing time by 60% and increased customer satisfaction scores significantly. The platform now handles hundreds of orders daily with minimal manual intervention.

## Key Features

### Responsive Design
The platform works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, ensuring customers can place orders from anywhere.

### Real-time Updates
Order status updates push to customers automatically via SMS and email, reducing support inquiries.

### Analytics Dashboard
Business owners can track sales trends, popular items, and customer behavior to make data-driven decisions.
